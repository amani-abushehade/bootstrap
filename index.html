Popular destinations in Palestine


Jerusalem is considered a holy city to which visitors from different parts of the world come for religious purposes. It includes the Al-Aqsa Mosque, the Dome of the Rock, the Church of the Holy Sepulcher, the Roman Chapel, the Church of Mary Magdalene, the Buraq Wall and many others. It includes many ancient houses, schools, monasteries, avenues, and other important archaeological and historical remains, and it is one of the oldest and holiest cities in the world.

Nablus is a Palestinian city located in the northern West Bank. The city contains a number of entertainment and archaeological places that can be visited. One of the most famous recreational places is the Wadi Al-Badhan area, which is a beautiful natural area and a suitable place for recreation and hiking for families and individuals. Sama Nablus, a 50-acre park located at the top of Mount Ebal, provides many recreational facilities for visitors. For lovers of historical tourism, the city contains many archaeological monuments, such as the Roman cemetery, the hippodrome, Jacob’s Well, as well as the amphitheater and theatre. It is necessary to visit Sebastia, which is considered an important archaeological treasure and is located 12 km from the city. The city also contains a number of parks and gardens, including Gamal Abdel Nasser Park, which belongs to the city’s municipality, and which provides the most beautiful and quiet sessions for everyone.

The city of Jaffa is located on the coast of the Mediterranean Sea. It enjoys a strategic geographical location that makes it one of the most beautiful cities on the coast. Its name means beauty or beautiful view. It is the ideal city for nature lovers and practicing water activities as well. Its beaches are considered among the most beautiful beaches in the world, and it is fully equipped for tourism. It is also spread over botanical and public gardens, and in addition to the natural ones, it includes the largest commercial centers in which there are various international brands, in addition to popular restaurants and international chains. It is worth noting that the city occupied the crossroads of transportation routes between the three continents of Asia, Europe and Africa, and its port is the oldest. Ports in the world.
